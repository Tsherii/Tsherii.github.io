about me

who am i?

I feel that life is an art form, and that I am the creator of my own life. Whatever life throws at me, I strive to keep it as colorful and bright as possible. I am an enthusiastic and inquisitive individual. Curious about what existence is all about and what ever the future holds.

I try to maintain a beaming smile on my face no matter what, and that smile is what motivates me. I am dependable yet often irritated. If I am not fully understood, I lose my cool. I'm furious on the inside, but it doesn't reflect in my actions. Throwing away on my dreams and desires was a natural consequence of my life. Living with little luxury and siblings had a significant impact on my character today.


Nevertheless, I never let my financial circumstances interfere with my academic ambitions. On the contrary, I've always been eager to acquire new knowledge and do well in school. As a reward, I was able to achieve admission to my preferred university on scholarship. Being admitted to college on a scholarship was both a dream and a major goal for me. Furthermore, it allowed me the chance to make my parents proud. Making them a proud parents is always been my ambition, and I couldn't be happier to see some of that dream come true.
